How to write a news article

Assembly time: 1-3 days
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering how to write a good news article? Maybe it's for a class or an extracurricular project? Or maybe you want to go into news writing? Follow the steps and you'll soon learn how to write a proper news article!

  • What to do and what you need

  • Decide on a news-related topic. Local? Breaking? Sports? etc.
  • Notepad for taking notes
  • Pencil or pen
  • Recording device (You can use your phone)
  • A computer to edit on
  • An extra set of eyes to read over your work

  1. Research your topic.

    Do your research on the topic you'll be covering and check multiple sources to confirm facts.

  2. Interviews.

    Decide who you're going to talk to and how many people you're going to talk to. How are they relevant to your story?

  3. Story length and interview questions.

    Decide the length of your story (approximately) and come up with relevant questions for an interview. 15-20 questions are recommended.

  4. How to structure your article.

    There are specific ways to structure any printed or professional article. Journalists follow the inverted pyramid and AP style format. To learn more about these formats in-depth, watch the video below.

  5. Draft your story.

    Draft an outline for your story once all the information and interviews are gathered and work on building your story from there. Don't forget to use the inverted pyramid and AP Style.

    Hands typing on a laptop.

  6. Have someone proof-read.

    After you've created a rough draft of your story, have a friend or perhaps a professor proof-read. A fresh set of eyes will always help and you'll be able to recieve feedback on your article.

  7. Add images and cite your sources.

    Once you're article is complete, add a couple of images related to the article to help the reader visualze while they read. Remember to credit where you got the photos from! It's important that you reference any external sources that you used in your article. Whether that be where you found certain information or people you interviewed that gave you information.

  8. Submit and publish your story.

    Now that you've followed all the steps and your article is complete, you can submit your article or publish it to article-publishing websites such as "Medium.com".
